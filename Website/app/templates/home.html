Who we are.

Our goal is to reduce the use of renewable resources, greenhouse gas emissions and the overall use of fueled vehicles. Through CityHire we aim to encourage everyone in the community to use a non-fueled vehicle whenever possible to travel around the beautiful city that is Leeds! Our costs are extremely affordable and we prioritize customer safety. So join us and book your first ride today!
